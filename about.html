ABOUT US

A Community Coming Together

The Bradford Creekwalk is more than a trail — it's a testament to what a community can achieve when united by a shared vision of preserving history and creating spaces that bring people together.

OUR STORY

From Vision to Reality

The Bradford Creekwalk began as a simple idea: transform an underutilized trail connecting downtown to the University into a vibrant recreational space that celebrates our community's history.

What started as a grassroots initiative has grown into a collaborative project involving historians, community leaders, volunteers, local businesses, the University, and residents passionate about preserving Bradford's heritage.

Today, the Creekwalk stands as a "museum without walls" — featuring 37 historical markers that span two centuries of our town's story, accessible to walkers, bikers, and strollers every day of the year.

"This trail isn't just about history — it's about building connections between generations, between town and campus, between past and future."

— Project Founder

NON-PROFIT MISSION

For the Community, By the Community

As a non-profit initiative, every donation, volunteer hour, and sponsorship goes directly toward maintaining and expanding the Creekwalk. We're committed to keeping this resource free and accessible to all.
