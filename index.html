ABOUT THE PROJECT

Where History Meets the Great Outdoors

The Bradford Creekwalk is an ambitious community initiative transforming a scenic trail that connects our historic downtown with the University campus into an immersive outdoor museum. Through 37 carefully curated historical markers, visitors journey through time while enjoying the natural beauty of our waterways and woodlands.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant, accessible outdoor space where Bradford's rich history comes alive, fostering community pride and connection while providing a beautiful recreational corridor that enhances the quality of life for residents and visitors alike.

Our Mission

To preserve and share Bradford's unique heritage through an innovative outdoor museum experience, creating a welcoming space for recreation, education, and community gathering that bridges our town and university while honoring our past and inspiring our future.
